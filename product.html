Ramayan

"Ramayan" is an ancient Indian epic, attributed to the sage Valmiki. It tells the story of Lord Rama, an incarnation of the god Vishnu, and his journey to rescue his wife Sita from the demon king Ravana. The epic explores themes of duty, honor, and the triumph of good over evil. It is revered for its moral teachings and has been a source of inspiration for generations. Its characters and episodes continue to hold significant cultural and religious importance in Hinduism.
